Direct products

Zm×Zn\mathbb{Z}_m\times \mathbb{Z}_nZm​×Zn​ is cyclic if and only if mmm and nnn have greatest common divisor 111.

More generally, for Zn1×Zn2×⋯×Znk\mathbb{Z}_{n_1}\times \mathbb{Z}_{n_2}\times \cdots \times \mathbb{Z}_{n_k}Zn1​​×Zn2​​×⋯×Znk​​, if n1,n2,⋯ ,nkn_1,n_2,\cdots,n_kn1​,n2​,⋯,nk​ are pairwise coprime, then the direct product is cyclic.

Define a relation on GGG and a∼ba\sim ba∼b if a−1b∈Ha^{-1}b\in Ha−1b∈H.
