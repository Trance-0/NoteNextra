Comparison test (convergence and divergence test (absolute convergence))

(b) If the series ∑n=1∞an\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}a_n∑n=1∞​an​ diverges, then the series ∑n=1∞cn\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}c_n∑n=1∞​cn​ diverges.
