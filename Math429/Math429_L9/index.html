Theorem 3.21 (The Fundamental Theorem of Linear Maps, Rank-nullity Theorem)

Suppose VVV is finite dimensional, and T∈L(V,W)T\in \mathscr{L}(V,W)T∈L(V,W), then range(T)range(T)range(T) is finite dimensional (WWW don’t need to be finite dimensional). and

dim(V)=dim(null(T))+dim(range(T))dim(V)=dim(null (T))+dim(range(T))dim(V)=dim(null(T))+dim(range(T))

Theorem 3.22

Let T∈L(V,W)T\in \mathscr{L}(V,W)T∈L(V,W) and suppose dim(V)>dim(W)dim(V)>dim(W)dim(V)>dim(W). Then TTT is not injective.

Proof:
