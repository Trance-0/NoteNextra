Definition 6.2

An inner product ⟨,⟩:V×V→F\langle,\rangle:V\times V\to \mathbb{F}⟨,⟩:V×V→F

Positivity: ⟨v,v⟩≥0\langle v,v\rangle\geq 0⟨v,v⟩≥0

Definiteness: ⟨v,v⟩=0  ⟺  v=0\langle v,v\rangle=0\iff v=0⟨v,v⟩=0⟺v=0

Additivity: ⟨u+v,w⟩=⟨u,w⟩+⟨v,w⟩\langle u+v,w\rangle=\langle u,w\rangle+\langle v,w\rangle⟨u+v,w⟩=⟨u,w⟩+⟨v,w⟩

Homogeneity: ⟨λu,v⟩=λ⟨u,v⟩\langle \lambda u, v\rangle=\lambda\langle u,v\rangle⟨λu,v⟩=λ⟨u,v⟩

Conjugate symmetry: ⟨u,v⟩=⟨v,u⟩‾\langle u,v\rangle=\overline{\langle v,u\rangle}⟨u,v⟩=⟨v,u⟩​

Note: the dot product on Rn\mathbb{R}^nRn satisfies these properties
