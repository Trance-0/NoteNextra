Properties of continuous functions

Definition of homeomorphism

A homeomorphism f:X→Yf:X\to Yf:X→Y is a continuous map of topological spaces that is a bijection and f−1:Y→Xf^{-1}:Y\to Xf−1:Y→X is also continuous.
