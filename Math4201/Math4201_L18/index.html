Quotient topology

Let (X,T)(X,\mathcal{T})(X,T) be a topological space and X∗X^*X∗ be a set, q:X→X∗q:X\to X^*q:X→X∗ is a surjective map. The quotient topology on X∗X^*X∗:

U⊆X∗U\subseteq X^*U⊆X∗ is open   ⟺  q−1(U)\iff q^{-1}(U)⟺q−1(U) is open in XXX.

Equivalently,

Z⊆X∗Z\subseteq X^*Z⊆X∗ is closed   ⟺  q−1(Z)\iff q^{-1}(Z)⟺q−1(Z) is closed in XXX.
