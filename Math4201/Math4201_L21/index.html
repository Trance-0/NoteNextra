Simplicial complexes

Recall from last lecture

Let σ={a0,a1,…,an}\sigma=\{a_0,a_1,\dots,a_n\}σ={a0​,a1​,…,an​} be a finite set. The nnn-dimensional simplex determined by τ\tauτ is given as:

Δn(a0,a1,…,an)={t0a0+t1a1+⋯+tnan∣ti≥0,∑i=0nti=1}\Delta^n(a_0,a_1,\dots,a_n)=\left\{t_0a_0+t_1a_1+\cdots+t_na_n\mid t_i\geq 0, \sum_{i=0}^n t_i=1\right\}Δn(a0​,a1​,…,an​)={t0​a0​+t1​a1​+⋯+tn​an​∣ti​≥0,i=0∑n​ti​=1}

If we have vertices τ={a0,a1,…,ak}\tau=\{a_0,a_1,\dots,a_k\}τ={a0​,a1​,…,ak​}, τ⊆σ\tau\subseteq \sigmaτ⊆σ, the face of Δn\Delta^nΔn is determined by τ\tauτ with dimension ∣τ∣−1|\tau|-1∣τ∣−1.
