Rigidity

Group

A set with a multiplication operator. (G,⋅)(G,\cdot)(G,⋅) such that: for all a,b,c∈Ga,b,c\in Ga,b,c∈G:

  1. a⋅b∈Ga\cdot b\in Ga⋅b∈G

  3. a⋅1=aa\cdot 1=aa⋅1=a

Ring

A group with two operations: addition and multiplication. (R,+,⋅)(R,+,\cdot)(R,+,⋅) such that: for all a,b,c∈Ra,b,c\in Ra,b,c∈R:

  1. Commutative under addition: a+b=b+aa+b=b+aa+b=b+a
  2. Associative under multiplication: (a⋅b)⋅c=a⋅(b⋅c)(a\cdot b)\cdot c=a\cdot (b\cdot c)(a⋅b)⋅c=a⋅(b⋅c)
  3. Distributive under addition: a⋅(b+c)=a⋅b+a⋅ca\cdot (b+c)=a\cdot b+a\cdot ca⋅(b+c)=a⋅b+a⋅c
