0≤∫‾abfdα−∫‾abfdα≤U(f,P,α)−L(f,P,α)<ϵ0 \leq \overline{\int}_a^b f d\alpha - \underline{\int}_a^b f d\alpha \leq U(f, P, \alpha) - L(f, P, \alpha) < \epsilon0≤∫​ab​fdα−∫​ab​fdα≤U(f,P,α)−L(f,P,α)<ϵ
