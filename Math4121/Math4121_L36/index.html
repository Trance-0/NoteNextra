Lemma Absolute Integrability

fff is integrable on EEE if and only if ∣f∣|f|∣f∣ is integrable on EEE and ∣∫Ef dm∣≤∫E∣f∣ dm\left|\int_E f \, dm\right|\leq \int_E |f| \, dm​∫E​fdm​≤∫E​∣f∣dm.

∫E∣f∣dm=∫E1f+dm+∫E2f−dm\int_E |f| dm=\int_{E_1} f^+ dm+\int_{E_2} f^- dm∫E​∣f∣dm=∫E1​​f+dm+∫E2​​f−dm
