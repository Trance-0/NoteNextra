Math4202 Topology II Exam 1 Practice

In the following, please provide complete proof of the statements and the answers you give. The total score is 25 points.

Problem 1

  • (2 points) State the definition of a topological manifold.

A topological manifold is a topological space that satisfies the following:

  1. It is Hausdorff
  2. It has a countable basis
  3. Each point of xxx of XXX has a neighborhood that is homeomorphic to an open subset of Rm\mathbb{R}^mRm.
  • (2 points) Prove that real projective space RP2\mathbb{R}P^2RP2 is a manifold.

Let RP2=R3/∼\mathbb{R}P^2=\mathbb{R}^3/\simRP2=R3/∼ where (x,y,z)∼(x′,y′,z′)(x,y,z)\sim(x',y',z')(x,y,z)∼(x′,y′,z′) if λ(x,y,z)=(x′,y′,z′)\lambda(x,y,z)=(x',y',z')λ(x,y,z)=(x′,y′,z′) for some λ∈R,λ≠0\lambda\in \mathbb{R},\lambda\neq 0λ∈R,λ=0.

  1. It is Hausdorff since R3\mathbb{R}^3R3 is Hausdorff, subspace of Hausdorff space is Hausdorff.
  2. It has a countable basis since R3\mathbb{R}^3R3 has a countable basis, subspace of countable basis has countable basis.
  3. Each point of xxx of RP2RP^2RP2 has a neighborhood that is homeomorphic to an open subset of R3\mathbb{R}^3R3. Let ppp be an arbitrary point in RP2RP^2RP2, Consider the projection on to the tangent plane of ppp defined as RP2→R2\mathbb{R}P^2\to \mathbb{R}^2RP2→R2.

The fundamental group of XXX relative to x0x_0x0​ is the group of all continuous paths from x0x_0x0​ to x0x_0x0​ under path homotopy equivalence.

The fundamental group of RnR^nRn relative to the origin is the trivial group.
