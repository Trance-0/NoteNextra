#### Proof of Seifert-Van Kampen Theorem

It is really long, but it turns that it is somehow "similar" to the proof of $S^2$ case.

Main idea is to cut the space into smaller pieces and decompose the loop into

$$
f=f_1*f_2*f_3*\ldots*f_n
$$

$$
\Phi(f)=\Phi_1(f_1)*\Phi_2(f_2)*\Phi_3(f_3)*\ldots*\Phi_n(f_n)
